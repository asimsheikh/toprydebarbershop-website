Welcome to Top Ryde Barber Shop

Top Ryde Barber Shop is a barber shop that provides high quality haircuts to all of its customers.

Customers can enjoy a haircut, beard trim or shave in a friendly pleasant environment.


The staff have been in the hair cutting business for over 10 years.

Customers are free to openly discuss politics, read the daily newspaper, or get a cup of coffee.


Everybody is welcome at the Top Ryde Barber Shop so please come and visit us.

Top Ryde Barber Shop is located at 4 Church Street Ryde NSW 2112
